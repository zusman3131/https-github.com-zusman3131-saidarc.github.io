Weapon Tips

01

Best Starter Loadout

Use Rattler as primary and Ferro as secondary. This gives you reliable full-auto and high-damage single shots.

02

ARC Killing Build

Bring a Hullcracker for heavy ARCs and an Equalizer or Ferro for general combat. Focus fire on weak points.

03

Ammo Efficiency

Bettina uses expensive Heavy Ammo. For budget runs, stick to Medium Ammo weapons like Tempest or Renegade.

04

Close Quarters Combat

In buildings and tight spaces, switch to SMGs or shotguns. The Bobcat and Vulcano excel at close range.

05

Long Range Engagements

The Osprey and Jupiter are your best options for long range. Remember: Osprey has a permanent scope.

06

The Hullcracker Secret

This grenade launcher ONLY damages ARCs, not players. Save it for Bastions, Commandos, and Boss enemies.
