Inventory Management

🎯 Smart Looting Priority

  1. Blueprints - Always your #1 priority
  2. Gun Parts - For crafting upgrades
  3. ARC Powercells - High value components
  4. Rare crafting materials - Arc Alloy, Processors
  5. Quest items - Marked with "Keep"

🎒 Safe Pocket Strategy

Your Safe Pocket guarantees return of one item even if you die.

    Perfect for quest items
  • Store rare components
  • Small valuable items only
  • Most weapons won't fit (too large)
  • Not available on Free Loadout runs

💡 Efficiency Tips

  • Prioritize high-rarity or high-yield crafting items
  • Recycle broken items for materials
  • Sell vendor trash for quick credits
  • Consider value per slot, not just total value
  • Drop low-value items for better finds

📦 What Fits in Safe Pocket

✓ Fits

  • Components & materials
  • Quest items
  • Small valuables
  • Keys

✗ Doesn't Fit

  • Most weapons
  • Large equipment
  • Backpacks

Best Farming Routes

Dam Battlegrounds

BEGINNER
Electrical Substations → Research & Admin → Hydroponic Dome

Great for batteries, basic materials, and plants. Low risk, consistent returns.

Buried City

INTERMEDIATE
Piazza Arbusto → Hospital → Night Crates

Excellent for accessories, unique items, and hospital loot. Higher PvP risk.

Spaceport

ADVANCED
Container Storage → Departure Building → Launch Tower

Best tech modules, schematics, and high-tier loot. Very high competition.

Blue Gate

EXPERT
Pilgrim's Peak → Village → Ancient Fort → Reinforced Reception

Best for power cores and high-grade electronics. Elite patrols, highest risk.
