What is Arc Raiders?

Arc Raiders is a cooperative extraction shooter set in a post-apocalyptic future. Teams of up to 3 players venture into the Rust Belt to scavenge valuable resources, battle deadly ARC machines, and extract safely with their loot.

Developed by Embark Studios (former Battlefield developers), the game combines intense PvPvE combat with deep crafting and progression systems. Every raid is a risk-reward decision — extract early with what you have, or push for more loot and risk losing everything.

Pro Tips

Essential advice from experienced raiders

01

Upgrade Your Crafting Table

Prioritize upgrading to Crafting Table Level 1 to unlock Looting Mk. 2 backpack. The two safe pocket slots are game-changing.

02

Target ARC Weak Points

Aim for the knees on most ARC units. This deals critical damage and can stagger them, giving you time to reposition.

03

Loot During Events

Night Raids and Hurricane events significantly increase loot quality. Farm locked rooms during these events for blueprints.

04

Use the Hullcracker Wisely

This grenade launcher only damages ARCs, not players. Save it for Bastions, Commandos, and Boss-tier enemies.

05

Communicate with Players

Most players are friendly. Use voice chat to coordinate extractions. Teaming up increases survival chances.

06

Don't Sell All Keys

Keys can be sold for 100 coins, but keeping them to farm locked rooms often yields much better rewards.
