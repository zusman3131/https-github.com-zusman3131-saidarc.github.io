Beginner's Survival Guide

🎯 Your First 10 Raids

1

Start on Dam Battlegrounds

This is the easiest map with manageable enemies and good loot. Learn the basics here before moving to harder maps.

2

Use Rattler + Ferro Loadout

The Rattler is cheap and reliable for close-to-mid range. The Ferro packs a punch for ARCs and long shots. Total cost: ~2,000 coins.

3

Bring 240 Medium + 120 Heavy Ammo

This gives you enough for extended fights without breaking the bank. Adjust based on your weapons.

4

Learn One Extraction Route

Pick one route (e.g., Control Tower → Water Treatment → North Extract) and run it until you know it by heart.

5

Prioritize Crafting Table Level 1

This unlocks the Looting Mk. 2 backpack with TWO safe pocket slots. Game changing for protecting valuable loot.

6

Aim for ARC Knees

The knees are weak points on most ARC units. Hitting them deals critical damage and can stagger the enemy.

7

Always Carry Smoke Grenades

Smoke is cheap and invaluable for extractions, reviving teammates, and escaping bad situations. Never leave base without at least 2.

8

Don't Sell Your Keys

Keys sell for 100 coins, but keeping them lets you farm locked rooms for much better rewards.

9

Extract With Something

A successful extract with basic loot is better than dying with a full backpack. Know when to walk away.

10

Use Voice Chat

Most players are friendly. Coordinating extractions, sharing loot, and teaming up against ARCs increases everyone's survival rate.

Combat Fundamentals

🎯 Player vs. Player

Listen First, Move Second

Audio cues are everything. Footsteps, gunfire direction, and ARC alert sounds give you crucial information.

Height Advantage

Always take the high ground when possible. Rooftops and upper floors give you better visibility and cover options.

Don't Chase

If you wound an enemy and they retreat, let them go. Chasing into unknown territory gets you killed.

Pre-nade Corners

When entering buildings, throw a frag or flash around blind corners. Better to waste a grenade than walk into an ambush.

🤖 Fighting ARCs

Target Weak Points

Aim for knees on humanoid ARCs. This deals critical damage and can stagger them, giving you time to reposition.

Use Cover

ARCs have predictable patterns. Pop out, shoot, duck back. Never stand in the open during ARC fights.

Bring the Hullcracker

For Bastions and heavy ARCs, the Hullcracker grenade launcher is essential. Remember: it only damages ARCs, not players.

Prioritize Targets

Kill Rocketeers and Wasps first (they deal the most damage), then clean up Ticks and Probes.

ARC Enemy Types

Know your enemy: threats and tactics

ARC Probe Low

Basic scout units. Weak but numerous. Go down quickly to any weapon fire.

Tactic: Any weapon works. Clear them quickly before they alert other ARCs.
ARC Wasp Low

Flying drones with ranged attacks. Fast but fragile.

Tactic: Use automatic weapons or aim carefully. Prioritize them in groups.
ARC Tick Medium

Small, fast melee units. They rush you and explode on contact.

Tactic: Keep distance, use automatic fire. One hit can ruin your raid.
ARC Rocketeer High

Heavy units with explosive projectiles. Deal massive damage from range.

Tactic:> Take cover immediately. Use snipers or the Hullcracker. Priority target.
ARC Bastion High

Heavily armored units with high health and strong attacks. Guard high-value areas.

Tactic: Hullcracker is essential. Focus fire with your team. Aim for weak points.
ARC Commando High

Elite units with advanced weapons and tactics. Rare but extremely dangerous.

Tactic: Team coordination required. Use cover, grenades, and the Hullcracker.
ARC Queen BOSS

Massive boss unit with devastating attacks and huge health pool. Spawns rarely.

Tactic: Full squad recommended. Hullcrackers, heavy weapons, and coordination essential. High rewards if defeated.

Loot Priority Guide

What to grab when inventory is limited

#1

Blueprints

Unlock new crafting recipes permanently. Extremely rare and valuable. Always goes in safe pockets.

#2

Keys

Access locked rooms with high-tier loot. Don't sell them (only 100 coins) — use them to farm better rewards.

#3

ARC Powercells

Rare crafting material for energy weapons and high-tier gear. Found from destroyed ARCs and power areas.

#4

Gun Parts

Essential for weapon crafting and upgrades. Common but always needed.

#5

Rare Attachments

Scopes, grips, and mods for your weapons. Purple/gold rarity attachments sell well or improve your guns.

#6

Valuable Sellables

Old World Goods, jewelry, electronics. Good for funding future runs when you need coins.

Recommended Progression

What to focus on as you advance

1

Early Game (Levels 1-5)

  • Learn Dam Battlegrounds inside and out
  • Unlock Crafting Table Level 1
  • Craft Looting Mk. 2 backpack
  • Find weapon Blueprints for Rattler, Ferro
  • Build up a coin reserve (50,000+)
2

Mid Game (Levels 6-15)

  • Unlock Buried City and Spaceport
  • Find Blueprints for Tempest, Renegade
  • Upgrade weapons to Mk. I variants
  • Start crafting better healing items
  • Learn PvP fundamentals
3

Late Game (Level 16+)

  • Unlock The Blue Gate
  • Find Legendary weapon Blueprints (Jupiter, Equalizer)
  • Upgrade Crafting Table to Level 2-3
  • Farm power cores and advanced electronics
  • Master all maps and extraction routes

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get more Blueprints?
Blueprints are found in locked rooms, safes, from boss ARCs, and during special events like Night Raids and Hurricanes. They can also be purchased from the Black Market (rotating stock).
What happens if I die in a raid?
You lose everything you brought in and everything you found, EXCEPT items in your safe pockets. This is why upgrading to the Looting Mk. 2 backpack (with 2 safe slots) is so important.
Can I play solo?
Yes! Solo play is viable, especially on Dam Battlegrounds. However, teaming up with other players (even randoms) significantly increases survival rates. Use voice chat to coordinate.
What's the best way to make money?
Efficient loot runs on Dam Battlegrounds are consistent. Extract with valuables, sell what you don't need, and avoid expensive deaths. Night Raids and Hurricane events have better loot but higher risk.
How do I unlock new maps?
Maps unlock as you level up your account. Dam Battlegrounds is available immediately, Buried City unlocks at level 5, Spaceport at level 10, and The Blue Gate at level 15.
What's the deal with the Hullcracker?
The Hullcracker is a grenade launcher that ONLY damages ARCs — not players. It's incredibly effective against Bastions, Commandos, and Boss ARCs. Essential for late-game content.
