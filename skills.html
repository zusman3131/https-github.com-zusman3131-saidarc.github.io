Skill Trees Breakdown

🏃

Mobility

HIGH PRIORITY

Marathon Runner

5 points

Reduces stamina cost when sprinting. Essential for quick escapes and faster map traversal.

FIRST SKILL TO GET

Youthful Lungs

5 points

Increases maximum stamina pool. Combined with Marathon Runner, you can sprint significantly longer.

GET THIS SECOND

Slide Distance

3 points

Increases slide distance. Nice for reaching cover faster but lower priority.

🎒

Survival

HIGH PRIORITY

In-Round Crafting

3 points

Allows crafting during raids. Make ammo, bandages, and utility items on the fly.

GAME CHANGER

Bandage Efficiency

3 points

Bandages heal more health per use. Helpful but lower priority than crafting.

Medicine Duration

3 points

Increases duration of healing items. Situational usefulness.

💪

Conditioning

MEDIUM PRIORITY

Used to the Weight

3 points

Better weight management. Move faster when carrying heavy loads.

Broad Shoulders

3 points

Increases carrying capacity. More loot per extraction means more profit.

Quick Holster

2 points

Faster weapon switching. Good for PvP encounters.

⚔️

Combat

MEDIUM PRIORITY

Reload Speed

3 points

Faster reloads. Useful for weapons with long reload animations.

Weapon Swap

2 points

Switch weapons faster in combat. Good for aggressive playstyles.

Aim Stability

3 points

Reduced weapon sway. Helpful for sniper rifles and marksman rifles.

🔧

Engineering

LOW PRIORITY

Crafting Efficiency

3 points

Reduced material costs for crafting. Nice for long-term savings.

Repair Kit Mastery

2 points

Better repair efficiency. Situationally useful.

🔍

Scavenging

LOW PRIORITY

Loot Detection

3 points

Detect loot from further away. Nice quality of life but not essential.

Loot Speed

2 points

Faster looting animations. Minor time save.

Skill Tips

01

Don't Spread Too Thin

Focus on maxing one tree at a time. Partial investments give diminishing returns.

02

Reset Costs Scale

Each skill reset costs more than the last. Plan your build before committing points.

03

Mobility Is King

Being able to escape bad situations is more valuable than slightly better combat stats.

04

In-Round Crafting Saves Runs

Running out of ammo mid-raid is a death sentence. This skill lets you craft more on the spot.
