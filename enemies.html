Enemy Database

Complete guide to all ARC threats in the Rust Belt

Threat Level:

Basic Enemies

Common threats you'll encounter frequently

🤖

Sentinel

Patrol Drone
LOW

Basic patrol drones that roam the maps in predictable patterns. The most common ARC encounter.

Weakness: Any weapon - weakest enemy type

Combat Tips

  • Patrol in predictable loops - learn their routes
  • Easy to eliminate solo
  • Sprinting attracts their attention
🕷️

Tick

Infiltrator
LOW-MED

Small indoor ARC units that grab onto players and deal continuous damage. Found primarily inside buildings.

Weakness: High rate-of-fire weapons

Combat Tips

  • Found indoors - check corners carefully
  • Quick bursts from SMGs or ARs work best
  • Stella Montis is excellent for Tick farming
🐝

Wasp

Flying Drone
LOW

Basic flying drones that attack from the air. Relatively weak but can be annoying in groups.

Weakness: Any weapon - aim for the body

Combat Tips

  • Track their flight pattern before shooting
  • Can spawn inside walls (bug) - be cautious
  • Prioritize when in groups
🔥

Firefly

Incendiary Drone
MEDIUM

Flying drones equipped with incendiary attacks. Deal fire damage over time and can be dangerous in enclosed spaces.

Weakness: Quick elimination, avoid fire zones

Combat Tips

  • Shoot quickly before they fire
  • Don't stand in fire patches
  • Patch 1.22.0: Reduced chance for multiples to spawn together

Medium Threats

Require attention and proper positioning

🦘

Leaper

Assault Unit
MEDIUM

Fast-moving ARC that jumps to close distance quickly. Can surprise players who aren't paying attention.

Weakness: Keep distance, shoot legs when they land

Combat Tips

  • Maintain distance - their jump has range
  • Shoot legs during landing animation
  • Don't let them corner you
🦟

Hornet

Aggressive Flyer
MEDIUM

Aggressive flying ARC that pursues players relentlessly. More durable than Wasps and deals more damage.

Weakness: Track and shoot - sustained fire

Combat Tips

  • Track their movement continuously
  • Don't stop shooting until they're down
  • Take cover if multiple are present
☄️

Comet

Fast Striker
MEDIUM

Fast-moving ground ARC that zips around the battlefield. Unpredictable movement makes them tricky to hit.

Weakness: Stun and burst (improved in Patch 1.22.0)

Combat Tips

  • Behavior is more predictable when stunned
  • Use area damage if available
  • Blue Gate Hurricane: Reduced spawn rate
🚀

Rocketeer

Missile Unit
MEDIUM-HIGH

Missile-launching ARC that attacks from range. High damage potential if their rockets connect.

Weakness: Close the distance, dodge rockets

Combat Tips

  • Close distance quickly - they're weaker up close
  • Strafe to dodge incoming missiles
  • Patch 1.22.0: Less collision damage, not instantly destroyed when stunned

Heavy Threats

Elite units requiring heavy weapons and coordination

💣

Bombardier

Artillery Unit
HIGH

Heavy artillery ARC that rains explosives from a distance. Devastating area denial capabilities.

Weakness: Shoot core/weak points, keep mobile

Combat Tips

  • Never stop moving - their AoE is lethal
  • Target the core/weak points for extra damage
  • Hullcracker grenade launcher is effective
🛡️

Bastion

Heavy Tank
HIGH

Heavily armored tank unit with massive health pool. Soaks up damage and requires sustained fire to eliminate.

Weakness: Hullcracker, aim for weak points

Combat Tips

  • Hullcracker grenade launcher is the best option
  • Aim for knee joints and core when exposed
  • Consider avoiding if under-equipped
⚙️

Shredder

Melee Destroyer
HIGH

Devastating melee-focused ARC added to all maps in Patch 1.22.0. Extremely dangerous at close range.

Weakness: Heavy weapons, maintain extreme distance

Combat Tips

  • NEVER let them get close - instant death zone
  • Use heavy weapons and explosives
  • Known bug: May float to ceiling in Hidden Bunker
☠️

Vaporizer

Elite Killer
VERY HIGH

NEW in Patch 1.22.0 - The most dangerous regular ARC. Devastating AoE attacks that can wipe squads.

Weakness: Unknown - avoid if possible

Combat Tips

  • AVOID unless you have coordinated squad and heavy weapons
  • Strategy still being developed by community
  • Report encounters to help map behavior

Boss Tier

Raid-ending threats requiring full squad coordination

👑

The Queen

BOSS

Massive boss-tier enemy with devastating attacks and enormous health pool. A raid-ending threat for unprepared squads.

Health Extreme
Damage Lethal
Speed Moderate

Strategy

  • Requires coordinated squad fire
  • Hullcracker essential for damage
  • Designate roles: damage, support, revive
  • Position for escape routes - don't get cornered
  • Worth the risk for legendary loot drops
🕸️

Matriarch

BOSS

Another boss-tier threat with unique attack patterns. Often spawns with smaller ARC escorts, making engagement more complex.

Health Extreme
Damage Lethal
Adds Yes

Strategy

  • Clear escorts first to simplify the fight
  • Coordinated fire from multiple angles
  • Hullcracker for main damage
  • Watch for special attack tells
  • High risk, high reward - legendary drops
