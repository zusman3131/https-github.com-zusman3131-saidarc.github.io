LOCATIONS

Explore the Maps

4 unique locations across the Rust Belt with detailed loot and enemy information

BEGINNER FRIENDLY

Dam Battlegrounds

Your first destination in the Rust Belt. The Dam Battlegrounds offers a balanced experience with manageable enemy density and reliable loot clusters. Perfect for learning extraction mechanics and combat fundamentals.

🔥 Key Points of Interest

  • Control Tower — High loot area with fewer ambush points on upper floors
  • Research & Administration — Excellent loot standing side-by-side with Control Tower
  • Hydroponic Dome Complex — Nature and Security loot, plenty of ARC patrols
  • Power Generation Complex — Solid loot with extraction point nearby
  • Testing Annex — Mid-tier loot, good for beginners
  • Water Treatment Control — Components for early crafting

💰 Loot Information

Gun Parts ARC Powercells Blueprints Components

Best for: Gun parts, crafting components, learning the game

🤖 Enemy Types

  • ARC Probes (Common)
  • ARC Wasps (Common)
  • ARC Ticks (Occasional)
  • ARC Rocketeers (Guarding POIs)
  • ARC Hornets (High loot areas)

🏃 Extraction Points

  • North Complex
  • South Complex
  • Near Power Generation

💡 Pro Tips

  • Start at the base of Control Tower, sweep Water Treatment, then extract via North Complex
  • The Controlled Access Zone requires 3 players to unlock — team up nearby or skip it
  • Ruby Residence, Pale Apartments, and Pattern House have the best key spawn rates
  • Hydroponic Dome has great ARC farming for crafting materials
INTERMEDIATE

Buried City

A compact urban environment designed for close-quarters combat. High PvP density and narrow streets create tense, fast-paced encounters. Perfect for learning PvP fundamentals.

🔥 Key Points of Interest

  • Piazza Roma — Loot-rich plaza with multiple buildings
  • Piazza Arbusto — Accessories and unique items in breachable buildings
  • Hospital — Great loot but often camped by players
  • Town Hall — Central location with multi-level combat
  • Santa Maria Houses — Rooftop attic access, weapon crates at night
  • Red Tower — Corners have security lockers
  • Space Travel Building — Key farming location
  • Grandioso Apartments — Residential loot area

💰 Loot Information

Accessories Unique Items Blueprints Old World Goods

Best for: High-value sellables, accessories, PvP practice

🤖 Enemy Types

  • ARC Wasps (Common)
  • ARC Rocketeers (Common)
  • ARC Bastions (High loot areas)
  • ARC Commandos (Occasional)
  • ARC Queen (Rare spawn)

🏃 Extraction Points

  • Marano Station
  • East Exit
  • Underground Passage

💡 Pro Tips

  • Clear rooftops before crossing alleys — snipers love this map
  • Pre-nade stairwells when entering buildings
  • Never extract without smoke or a buddy watching rooftops
  • Hospital has the best loot but expect heavy PvP
  • Marano Station stash loop is fast and lucrative
ADVANCED

Spaceport

A high-tech facility with the highest loot ceiling in the game. The Launch Tower and terminal clusters offer top-tier rewards, but fierce competition and elite ARC patrols make this map challenging.

🔥 Key Points of Interest

  • Launch Tower — Highest loot potential, highest risk
  • Container Storage — Excellent crates but fierce competition
  • Departure Building — Lockers with great loot, especially at night
  • Control Tower A6 — Security lockers on west side
  • Shipping Warehouse — Mid-tier loot, good for scavenging
  • Fuel Lines — Zipline access to rooftop crates
  • Arrival Building — South side has breachable doors
  • Medical Area — Safes and large amount of loot
  • North/South Trench Tower — Key-required high-value rooms

  • Use the "Switching the Supply" quest to get Trench Tower keys instead of farming
  • Departure Building lockers are amazing at night — bring NVGs
  • Hullcracker is essential here for dealing with Bastions and Commandos
  • Watch for the Leaper in underground bunker areas
ADVANCED

The Blue Gate

Elite ARC patrols and dangerous surface areas make this the most challenging map. Underground tunnels offer safer passage while surface areas contain the best loot. Ideal for farming power cores and advanced electronics.

🔥 Key Points of Interest

  • Raider's Refuge — Southwest corner, good starting point
  • Maintenance Bunker — Underground corridor with loot
  • Reinforced Reception — Zipline down to security lockers
  • Pilgrimage's Peak — Northeast corner POI
  • The Village — Top-left buildings have key spawns
  • Container Storage Top Floor — Requires Spaceport key

💰 Loot Information

Power Cores Advanced Electronics Blueprints Endgame Gear

Best for: Late-game upgrades, power cores, high-grade electronics

🤖 Enemy Types

  • ARC Sentinels (Surface patrols)
  • ARC Surveyors (High alert)
  • ARC Bombardiers (Guarding POIs)
  • ARC Hornets (Swarms)
  • ARC Queen (High chance)
  • ARC Matriarch (Event spawns)

🏃 Extraction Points

  • Raider Hatch (Multiple locations)
  • Supply Call Station Extracts
  • Emergency Bunker Exit

💡 Pro Tips

  • Use the underground tunnels instead of crossing the dangerous surface
  • Bring your best gear — this map demands it
  • Coordinate with your team — solo play is extremely risky here
  • Farm during Night Raids or Hurricane events for increased blueprint drops
  • Raider Hatches require keys but provide the safest extraction
