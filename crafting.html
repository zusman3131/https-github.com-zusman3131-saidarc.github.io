Crafting Guide

Master the workbench: recipes, materials, and upgrade progression

How Crafting Works

Arc Raiders features a deep crafting system that lets you upgrade your equipment, improve your loadouts, and unlock new abilities. Everything happens at your Workbench in the lobby.

🔧

Workbench Access

Find the Workbench in the lobby to start crafting. Use the Fabricator for consumables.

📋

Blueprint Unlocks

Crafting requires Blueprints found in raids. All Blueprints unlock for your account permanently.

⬆️

Upgrade Progression

Most items require previous tiers. Build a Bettina Mk. I before you can craft Mk. II.

🎯

First Priority

Upgrade your Crafting Table to Level 1 immediately to unlock Looting Mk. 2 backpack.

Crafting Tiers

Upgrade your table to unlock better recipes

  • Basic pistols
  • Mk. 0 backpacks
  • Basic grenades
  • Common weapons

Material Sources

Where to find common crafting materials

Scrap Common
Source: Junk piles, broken equipment, loot containers
Used in: Almost everything — stock up!
Mechanical Parts Common
Source: Machinery, vehicles, industrial areas
Used in: Weapons, backpacks, attachments
Electronic Components Uncommon
Source: Computers, terminals, electronic devices
Used in: Tech weapons, scopes, advanced gear
Circuit Boards Uncommon
Source: Security rooms, server rooms, Control Towers
Used in: Weapon upgrades, tech equipment
ARC Powercells Rare
Source: Destroyed ARCs, power stations, high-tier loot
Used in: Energy weapons, high-tier gear
Gun Parts Uncommon
Source: Weapon crates, gun shops, armories
Used in: Weapon crafting and upgrades
Backpack Frame Rare
Source: Military crates, supply caches, quest rewards
Used in: All backpack crafting
Blueprints Rare
Source: Locked rooms, safes, boss ARCs, events
Used in: Unlocking craftable items (consumed on unlock)
